Play Fair

Server Rules

Follow these rules to keep the server fun and fair for everyone. Violations may result in a ban. 📋

#1
Be Respectful
Treat all players with kindness. No harassment, hate speech, or bullying of any kind.
#2
No Cheating or Hacking
No hacked clients, X-ray, or exploits. Keep the game fair for everyone.
#3
No Griefing
Do not destroy, steal from, or damage other players' builds without permission.
#4
Keep Chat Clean
No spamming, excessive caps, NSFW content, or advertising other servers.
#5
Listen to Staff
Respect and follow directions from moderators and admins at all times.
#6
Have Fun!
The most important rule — enjoy yourself and help make this a great place for all.
